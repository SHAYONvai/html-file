Marks: 5 • Question No. 7

Introduction

Narration পরীক্ষায় আসে ৭ দাগে। Direct থেকে Indirect অথবা Indirect থেকে Direct করতে বলা হয়। Narration এ ৫ এর মধ্যে ৫ পাওয়ার জন্য Sentence এবং Tense সবথেকে ভালোভাবে জানতে হবে। Reporting Verb ও অন্যান্য General rules অবশ্যই টানা মুখস্থ থাকতে হবে। বিগত বছরের Board Question Practise এর কোনো বিকল্প নেই।

2 Sentence Structures & Examples

Assertive

Sub + Verb + Obj

Ex: I love my country.

Interrogative

Aux + Sub + Main Verb + Obj + ?

Ex: Is he reading now? / Did he attend the class?

Imperative

Verb + Obj

Ex: Do the work.

Optative

May + Sub + Verb + Obj

Ex: May Allah bless you.

Exclamatory

What/How + Adj + Noun + Sub + Verb + !

Ex: How beautiful the sky is!

3 Direct to Indirect Examples

Assertive

Dir: He said to me, "I like coffee".

Ind: He told me that he liked coffee.

Interrogative (Yes/No)

Dir: Rini said to me, "Are you going to Dhaka?".

Ind: Rini asked me if I was going to Dhaka.

Interrogative (WH)

Dir: She said to me, "Where have you gone?".

Ind: She asked me where I had gone.

Imperative

Dir: I said to him, "Give me a cup of coffee".

Ind: I ordered him to give me a cup of coffee.

Dir: The teacher said to me, "Don't tell a lie."

Ind: The teacher advised me not to tell a lie.

Optative

Dir: Father said to me, "May Allah bless you".

Ind: Father wished that Allah might bless me.

Exclamatory

Dir: She said, "What a beautiful bird it is."

Ind: She exclaimed with joy that it was a very beautiful bird.

Person Change Rules

  • 1
    1st Person (I, we) changes according to Subject of reporting verb.
  • 2
    2nd Person (you, your) changes according to Object of reporting verb.
  • 3
    3rd Person (he, she, it, names) - No Change.

General Rules

  • Yes: replied in the affirmative
  • No: replied in the negative
  • Addressing someone: Addressing as...
  • Sir: respectfully / with due respect
  • Multiple Sentences: Assertive: said/told → added → further added
    Interrogative: asked → also asked

Special Phrases

Thank You

Sub + thanked + obj
Dir: He said to me, "Thank you."
Ind: He thanked me.

Good Bye

bade + sub + good bye
Dir: She said to me, "Good bye."
Ind: She bade me good bye.

Greetings (Good morning/evening)

wished + sub + good morning...
Dir: He said to me, "Good morning."
Ind: He wished me good morning.
